Web Development

Welcome to the JFK Web Development class! Throughout the year we will learn to use HTML, CSS, and Javascript to design and develop engaging websites. We will explore how the World Wide Web works, basic design principles, and how to create dynamic web pages. By the end of the year you will have a portfolio full of interesting projects we've worked on.
